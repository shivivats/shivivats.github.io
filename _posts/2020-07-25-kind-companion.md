Kind Companion is an Android app developed to **help people deal with anxiety**. I developed it alone over a course of 3 months as part of my internship at my [university](https://www.aau.at/en/). I also used this project as a way to learn how to make Android apps in **Android Studio** using **JAVA** and **XML**.

Some functionalities implemented in the app are:

- Room Database with LiveData and ViewModel
- Audio Recorder, Camera and Image Chooser
- Custom Alert Dialogues
- Orientation-specific Layouts
