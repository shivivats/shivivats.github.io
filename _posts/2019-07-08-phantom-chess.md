Phantom Chess is an **auto-chess** game built for [Geta Game Jam 8](https://itch.io/jam/geta-game-jam-8). The player plays against the AI and tries to reach the highest level they can. The game goes on till the player loses. It functions as a regular auto-chess game where the player can buy/sell units, upgrade them via combining 3, etc.

The game was built using the **Unity3D** engine and written in **C#**. I worked in a team of two on this, as a **gameplay programmer**. I programmed the combat, movement and combination functionalities that were core to the game, among other features.
